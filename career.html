Career Options

Below are some of the possible jobs that you are able to work in after graduation from DAAA. Do note that the data below is only an estimate/average and may not be the exect same from person to person

↓

Data Analyst

  • Uses existing data to help solve tangible business problems
  • establish how data categories are related to each other, and work through how the data actually appears.
  • Interpreting data will involve finding patterns or trends in data that will help you answer the question at hand.
  • Tableau, Jupyter Notebook, and Excel are among the many tools used

Avr base pay $62,382 (Glassdoor)

Data Scientist

  • Finds new ways to collect and analyze data
  • Create algorithms and data models to forecast outcomes
  • Uncover solutions to business challenges.
  • Some of the software frameworks used to process big data include Hadoop and Apache Spark.
  • They're part mathematician, part computer scientist and part trend-spotter.

Avr base pay $83,920 (Glassdoor)

Artificial Intelligence Engineer

  • Develops Artificial intelligence tool that are more efficient and have better performance
  • Conduct statistical analysis and interpret the results to guide and optimize the organization’s decision-making process
  • Transform machine learning models into APIs that can be integrated
  • Collaborate across teams to help with AI adoption and best practices

Avr base pay $96,242 (Glassdoor)
